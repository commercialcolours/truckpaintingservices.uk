Commercial truck painting services

Perhaps you’re looking for a simple panel respray, dent or scratch repair or respraying, or even a full body overhaul? What should you look for in a professional truck painting service?

  • All makes and models should be catered for, from small trucks right up to the largest commercial vehicles.
  • They should use the highest quality products, have the latest facilities and skilled technicians with years of experience.
  • The company should be EPA registered and approved by the Office of Fair Trading.

Commercial truck painting company

A commercial truck painting company will offer all types of paint finishes and use only high-quality products. They should match paint to manufacturers’ swatches using computerised paint mixing systems which will produce an exact colour match of all commercial vehicles.
